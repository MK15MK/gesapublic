Office Containers

Whether you require additional office space on a temporary or permanent basis, We at Gesa Containers manufacture magnificent office containers for our clients at cost-effective prices. These types of containers are portable workstations that prove to be an ideal choice for people who’re looking for offices or crew cabins in a snap.

These containers are well-equipped with all contemporary facilities to serve the purpose of office environment. We design and produce these according to our customers’ specifications in respect to space, size, shape, fitting, amenities, etc. and make sure we comply to top grade quality standard and provide praiseworthy output.
We offer professional customization of each office container unit to meet your specific needs.

For example, our expert team can add windows, shelving, carpeting, electrical points, lighting fixtures, extra security features and other amenities according to your specifications.

Units are also available with or without insulation and air conditioning. All conversion work is done on our premises, ensuring that once we deliver an office container, you can begin using it for the intended purpose immediately.

8′ x 20′ all office containers.
